What is HUI?

The Hawaii Undergraduate Initiative (HUI) is a summer leadership program providing a free 3-credit summer college course for a select group of incoming freshmen at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. In addition to the summer course, HUI offers engaging and insightful workshops on using university resources, exploring academics/careers and developing writing/math skills. All HUI participating scholars will be paired with a Peer Mentor who will support and assist them in their transition from high school to college.

Applicant Eligibility

Interested students must be a Hawaii high school senior graduating the current school year AND be accepted to and plan on attending the University of Hawaii at Manoa, meeting the following requirements:

  • Applicants must be Fall 2024 incoming UHM freshmen
  • Available to participate in the HUI Summer Program from July 1 - August 9, 2024, 9:00 am - 12:00 pm
  • Priority given to students who meet one of the following: graduate from a Hawai'i Title 1 school, be Pell Grant eligible as determined by FAFSA, first-generation to college, or be a member of an underrepresented community at UHM.

Our Peer Mentors

Students will be grouped with peer mentors who support and assist them in their transition. Each peer mentor session focuses around a topic and students will engage in various activities surrounding that topic. Students will discuss transitional topics to ease their anxieties and their transition prior to the start of their first day. In addition students will debrief about each topic and have the opportunity to put these topics into action. These peer mentors will typically be in the same field/interest as the student and will be there to guide them through the summer and academic school year. Our current PMs are listed below. Resource Workshops

Resource Workshops are designed to help students connect to some of the resources on campus to facilitate their transition to the university. These resource workshops cover a topic and discusss ways students can get involved with that resource. Examples of previous resource workshops we've held include:

  • Warrior Recreation Center Tours
  • Hamilton Library Tours
  • Manoa Career Center
  • Financial Aid
  • Learning Assistance Center
  • LGBTQ+
  • PAU Violence
  • Counseling and Student Development
  • Study Abroad Program
  • ... and many more!
